Chapter Overview: Ranji’s Wonderful Bat

English Overview:
"Ranji’s Wonderful Bat" by Ruskin Bond is an inspiring story about a young boy named Ranji who struggles with his cricket performance. After failing in several matches, he feels discouraged. Mr. Kumar, a sports shop owner and former state-level player, lends him an "old, lucky bat" that he claims helped him score a century. Believing in the magic of the bat, Ranji gains confidence and starts scoring well. Later, when he loses the bat, Mr. Kumar reveals the truth: the magic was never in the bat, but in Ranji’s own self-belief and confidence. The story teaches us that success comes from believing in one's own abilities.
