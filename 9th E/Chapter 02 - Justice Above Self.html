Chapter Overview

English Overview: "Justice Above Self" by Munshi Premchand is a poignant story about the friendship between two villagers, Jumman Shaikh and Algu Chowdhary. The narrative explores the conflict between personal relationships and the demands of justice. When Jumman's aunt seeks justice against him in a Panchayat, Algu is chosen as the head Panch. Despite their close friendship, Algu delivers a fair verdict against Jumman, prioritizing truth over personal ties. Later, when Algu faces a similar situation with a bullock sale, Jumman, serving as the Panch, reciprocates this integrity. The story emphasizes that the voice of a Panch is the voice of God, and justice must remain impartial.
