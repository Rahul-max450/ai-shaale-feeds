Chapter Overview: Monday Morning

English Overview:
"Monday Morning" is an excerpt from Mark Twain's classic novel, "The Adventures of Tom Sawyer." The story captures the mischievous and imaginative nature of a young boy named Tom Sawyer, who desperately wants to avoid going to school on a Monday morning. Tom attempts to feign illness, searching for symptoms of various ailments, eventually settling on a "sore toe." He tries to wake his half-brother, Sid, to get attention, and eventually succeeds in alarming his Aunt Polly. The story highlights the humorous interaction between Tom and his aunt, culminating in the extraction of his loose tooth. It serves as a lighthearted look at childhood reluctance toward school and the clever, albeit dramatic, ways children try to escape their responsibilities.
