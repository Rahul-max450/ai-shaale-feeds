Chapter Overview: My Beginnings - Kapil Dev

English Overview:
"My Beginnings" is an excerpt from the autobiography "Straight from the Heart" by the legendary Indian cricketer Kapil Dev. The chapter provides a nostalgic look into his childhood in Chandigarh. Kapil shares anecdotes about his early life, his mischievous nature, his love for outdoor activities, and his initial interest in various sports like football and athletics. He recounts humorous incidents, such as stealing pomegranates from a neighbor's garden and attempting to bring a police horse home. The narrative highlights how his friends influenced him to switch to cricket, a decision that eventually led him to become one of India's greatest all-rounders and the captain who led the team to the 1983 World Cup victory.
