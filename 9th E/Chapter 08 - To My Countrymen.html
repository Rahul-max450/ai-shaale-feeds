Chapter Overview

English Overview: "To My Countrymen" is an inspiring extract from Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam's book 'Ignited Minds'. In this chapter, Dr. Kalam emphasizes the power of imagination and the necessity of having a clear vision for the nation. He urges the youth of India to dream big, work with integrity, and take responsibility for the country's development. He highlights the importance of integrated efforts across various sectors—science, technology, and industry—to transform India into a developed nation. He also stresses the need for education, self-reliance in manufacturing, and a shift in attitude from "What can I take?" to "What can I give?"
