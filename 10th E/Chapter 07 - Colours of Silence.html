CHAPTER OVERVIEW: COLOURS OF SILENCE

English Overview

The chapter "Colours of Silence" tells the inspiring story of Satish Gujral, a boy who loses his hearing due to an accident and subsequent medical complications. Initially, Satish struggles with isolation, depression, and the inability to attend school. However, he discovers a passion for painting and sketching, which becomes his solace. Despite his father's initial opposition, Satish's talent and persistence eventually win him over. The story highlights how physical disability is not a barrier to success and how Satish overcomes his challenges to become one of India's most renowned artists.
