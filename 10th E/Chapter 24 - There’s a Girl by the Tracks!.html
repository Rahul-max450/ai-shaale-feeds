Chapter Overview

English Overview

“There’s a Girl by the Tracks!” by Deven Kanal is a gripping narrative about a 21-year-old call centre executive, Roma Talreja, who accidentally falls from a moving suburban train in Mumbai. The story highlights the indifference of the public and the heroic, selfless act of Baleshwar Mishra, an unemployed youth who jumps off a moving train to save her. Despite facing apathy from bystanders and logistical challenges, Baleshwar manages to get Roma to a hospital, ultimately saving her life. The chapter emphasizes the values of humanity, compassion, and the courage to act in a crisis.
