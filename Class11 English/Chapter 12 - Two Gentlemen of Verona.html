Chapter Overview: Two Gentlemen of Verona

English Overview:
"Two Gentlemen of Verona" by A.J. Cronin is a poignant story set in the aftermath of World War II in the Italian city of Verona. It follows two young brothers, Nicola and Jacopo, who work tirelessly at various odd jobs—selling fruit, shining shoes, and acting as guides—to support their sister, Lucia, who is suffering from tuberculosis of the spine in a hospital. The narrator, initially skeptical of their shabby appearance, eventually discovers their noble secret: they are sacrificing their own childhood and comfort to pay for their sister's medical treatment. The story highlights themes of resilience, selfless love, and the dignity of human spirit in the face of war's devastation.
